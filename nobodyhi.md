Below is a quick-reference list of **30 versatile question structures** you can plug into everyday conversations, research, or problem-solving.  
For each item you’ll see:

-   **Scenario** – When you’d typically ask it
    
-   **Structure (template)** – The fill-in-the-blank form
    
-   **Example** – One concrete use
    

* * *

### 1\. Definition / Description

-   **Scenario:** You need a plain-language explanation of a term or object.
    
-   **Structure:** _What is \_\_\_?_ / _What are \_\_\_?_
    
-   **Example:** “What is **blockchain governance**?”
    

### 2\. Acronym Expansion

-   **Scenario:** You stumble on an unfamiliar abbreviation.
    
-   **Structure:** _What does \_\_\_ stand for?_
    
-   **Example:** “What does **DAO** stand for?”
    

### 3\. Mechanism / Process Explanation

-   **Scenario:** Curious how something actually works under the hood.
    
-   **Structure:** _How does \_\_\_ work?_
    
-   **Example:** “How does **Proof-of-Stake** work?”
    

### 4\. Step-by-Step Instructions

-   **Scenario:** You want a practical, ordered guide.
    
-   **Structure:** _How do I \_\_\_?_
    
-   **Example:** “How do I **deploy a smart contract on Ethereum?**”
    

### 5\. Cause / Reason

-   **Scenario:** You’re investigating why something happens.
    
-   **Structure:** _Why does \_\_\_ happen?_ / _Why is \_\_\_?_
    
-   **Example:** “Why does **metal rust**?”
    

### 6\. Root Causes

-   **Scenario:** Diagnosing problems or phenomena.
    
-   **Structure:** _What causes \_\_\_?_
    
-   **Example:** “What causes **network latency spikes**?”
    

### 7\. Comparison (Differences)

-   **Scenario:** Evaluating two options or concepts.
    
-   **Structure:** _What is the difference between \_\_\_ and \_\_\_?_
    
-   **Example:** “What is the difference between **IPFS** and **Arweave**?”
    

### 8\. Comparison (Similarities)

-   **Scenario:** Finding common ground.
    
-   **Structure:** _How is \_\_\_ similar to \_\_\_?_
    
-   **Example:** “How is **Rust** similar to **C++**?”
    

### 9\. Pros & Cons

-   **Scenario:** Weighing benefits versus drawbacks.
    
-   **Structure:** _What are the pros and cons of \_\_\_?_
    
-   **Example:** “What are the pros and cons of **remote work**?”
    

### 10\. “Best” Recommendation

-   **Scenario:** Seeking the top choice for a need.
    
-   **Structure:** _What is the best \_\_\_ for \_\_\_?_
    
-   **Example:** “What is the best **laptop** for **Solidity development**?”
    

### 11\. General Recommendation

-   **Scenario:** Looking for any suitable option.
    
-   **Structure:** _Can you recommend a \_\_\_ that \_\_\_?_
    
-   **Example:** “Can you recommend a **DAW** that works well on Linux?”
    

### 12\. Requesting Examples

-   **Scenario:** Need concrete illustrations.
    
-   **Structure:** _Can you give me examples of \_\_\_?_
    
-   **Example:** “Can you give me examples of **privacy-preserving protocols**?”
    

### 13\. Meaning / Interpretation

-   **Scenario:** Unsure what a word or phrase conveys.
    
-   **Structure:** _What does \_\_\_ mean?_
    
-   **Example:** “What does **‘gas fee’** mean in Ethereum?”
    

### 14\. Usage in Context

-   **Scenario:** Learning how to apply a tool or term.
    
-   **Structure:** _How do you use \_\_\_ in \_\_\_?_
    
-   **Example:** “How do you use **async/await** in **Python**?”
    

### 15\. Timing / Schedule

-   **Scenario:** Clarifying when something occurs.
    
-   **Structure:** _When does \_\_\_ happen?_ / _What time does \_\_\_ start?_
    
-   **Example:** “When does **Devcon 2025** start?”
    

### 16\. Location / Availability

-   **Scenario:** Searching for a place or resource.
    
-   **Structure:** _Where can I find \_\_\_?_ / _Where is \_\_\_ located?_
    
-   **Example:** “Where can I find **the Goerli faucet?**”
    

### 17\. Cost Inquiry

-   **Scenario:** Budgeting or price comparison.
    
-   **Structure:** _How much does \_\_\_ cost?_
    
-   **Example:** “How much does **an ENS .eth name** cost?”
    

### 18\. Possibility / Feasibility

-   **Scenario:** Checking if something can be done.
    
-   **Structure:** _Is it possible to \_\_\_?_
    
-   **Example:** “Is it possible to **run GPT models locally on an M-series Mac?**”
    

### 19\. Permission

-   **Scenario:** Asking if an action is allowed.
    
-   **Structure:** _May I \_\_\_?_ / _Can I \_\_\_?_
    
-   **Example:** “Can I **use GPL-licensed code in a commercial project?**”
    

### 20\. Probability / Likelihood

-   **Scenario:** Gauging chances or risks.
    
-   **Structure:** _What are the chances that \_\_\_?_
    
-   **Example:** “What are the chances that **ETH gas fees drop below 5 gwei this year?**”
    

### 21\. Consequences / What-If

-   **Scenario:** Anticipating outcomes.
    
-   **Structure:** _What will happen if \_\_\_?_
    
-   **Example:** “What will happen if **I lose my hardware wallet?**”
    

### 22\. Either-Or Decision

-   **Scenario:** Choosing between two paths.
    
-   **Structure:** _Should I \_\_\_ or \_\_\_?_
    
-   **Example:** “Should I **learn Rust** or **Go** for blockchain back-end work?”
    

### 23\. Value / Worth

-   **Scenario:** Deciding if something merits investment.
    
-   **Structure:** _Is \_\_\_ worth it?_
    
-   **Example:** “Is **the MacBook Pro M4** worth it for AI developers?”
    

### 24\. Future Prediction

-   **Scenario:** Looking ahead in time.
    
-   **Structure:** _What will \_\_\_ be like in \_\_\_?_
    
-   **Example:** “What will **Layer-2 scaling** be like in **2030**?”
    

### 25\. Clarification

-   **Scenario:** When an explanation needs more detail.
    
-   **Structure:** _Could you clarify what you mean by \_\_\_?_
    
-   **Example:** “Could you clarify what you mean by **‘progressive decentralization’?**”
    

### 26\. Alternatives

-   **Scenario:** Seeking replacements or options.
    
-   **Structure:** _What are some alternatives to \_\_\_?_
    
-   **Example:** “What are some alternatives to **MetaMask**?”
    

### 27\. Procedure / Policy

-   **Scenario:** Navigating official steps.
    
-   **Structure:** _What is the procedure for \_\_\_?_
    
-   **Example:** “What is the procedure for **submitting an EIP?**”
    

### 28\. Quantity / Measurement

-   **Scenario:** Figuring out amounts.
    
-   **Structure:** _How many \_\_\_ are there?_ / _How much \_\_\_ is needed?_
    
-   **Example:** “How many **validators** does Ethereum currently have?”
    

### 29\. Frequency

-   **Scenario:** Establishing routine or schedule.
    
-   **Structure:** _How often should I \_\_\_?_
    
-   **Example:** “How often should I **update my npm dependencies?**”
    

### 30\. Responsibility

-   **Scenario:** Identifying who’s in charge.
    
-   **Structure:** _Who is responsible for \_\_\_?_
    
-   **Example:** “Who is responsible for **maintaining the Solidity compiler?**”
    

* * *

Feel free to adapt or combine these templates to suit specific contexts—the blanks are there for your creativity!
